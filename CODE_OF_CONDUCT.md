# Code of Conduct

## Our standards

- Be direct, respectful, and technical.
- Assume good intent and focus feedback on code and behavior.
- Keep bug reports and reviews concrete and reproducible.
- Do not post private credentials, private logs, or exploit details in public threads.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, threats, or personal attacks
- Doxxing or sharing credentials
- Spam, trolling, or deliberately disruptive behavior

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, lock, or remove content that violates this code of conduct and may block repeat offenders.

For conduct or security concerns that should not be public, use the process in [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md).
